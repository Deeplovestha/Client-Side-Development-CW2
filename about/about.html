About SpeedRide

Where Passion Meets Performance

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by DIPLOB SHRESTHA, a lifelong bike enthusiast, SpeedRide Showroom began as a small garage workshop with a simple vision: to bring the thrill of premium bikes to UK. We started with just 3 models has now grown into UK largest premium bike dealership, serving over 100+ satisfied customers.

We're not just sellers - we're riders ourselves, and we understand what makes a great biking experience. From your first commuter bike to your dream superbike, we've been there with our customers through every mile of their journey.
